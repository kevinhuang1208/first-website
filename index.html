Everyone's First Impression of Boston

If you're not from Boston, you will never know how it feels like to be assumed that you have a Boston accent, or use the term "wicked" in every sentence we say. Not cool... BUT, I do have to admit, sometimes, and I do mean sometimes because it is very situational, it feels good to say "wickEDDD" to something real hype or "pahked the cah in hahvud yahd" when imitating a "Bostonian". Only we get those privileges though :). Anywhom, this website will tell you that we have more than just that. We are a diverse and friendly group of people who will help you out if you're a quarter short for the train (I will, at least). Sit back and enjoy!
